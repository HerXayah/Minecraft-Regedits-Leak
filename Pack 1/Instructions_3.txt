REGEDIT INSTALATION 

1. Extract the regedit onto your desktop.

2. Double click on the regedit, and press allow for each instruction. 

LATENCY METHOD 

1. Press the arrow on the bottom right of your taskbar as displayed here; https://prnt.sc/ifwcd2

2. Right click on this icon; https://prnt.sc/ifwcpg and select "Open Network & Internet Settings"

3. Scroll down and select Network and Sharing, then click on the wifi or ethernet you are currently connected to.\

4. Click Properties.

5. Go to Internet Protocal Version 4 (TCP/IPv4)

6. Click on Advanced

7. Enter 1000 into the interface metric box located at the bottom.

8. Press OK and Apply!
